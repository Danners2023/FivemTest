About FiveM

FiveM is a modification for the popular open-world game Grand Theft Auto V that allows players to create and join custom multiplayer servers. It offers a wide range of features and gameplay improvements, making it a popular choice for players looking to enhance their GTA V experience.

Features

  • Custom game modes
  • Custom weapons and vehicles
  • Custom maps and environments
  • Player-run organizations and communities
